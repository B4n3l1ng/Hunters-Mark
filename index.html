Hunter's Mark

Welcome to Hunter's Mark

Your village is under attack by a horde of orcs, goblins and ogres!
Take up arms with your trusty bow against the onslaught of enemies and save your village!

Use the up and down keys on your keyboard to move our ranger hero, Cris.
Pressing the space bar will cause her to shoot an arrow in front of her. If an arrow hits an orc, the orc will die.
Don't let them kill you, or reach the village behind you, for they will kill everything in their path!
Press the V key to summon your companions if you're feeling overwhelmed, but remember, you can only do it once per try!
If you manage to kill all 50 of your enemies, you save the village!
